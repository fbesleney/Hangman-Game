HANGMAN

Find the hidden word!

How to Play


Welcome, the aim of the game is to find a hidden word.

Click the "start" button to start the game. Click the X button to exit the popup window. On the keyboard, press the letters you think can be found in the word. Correct letters will be displayed in the underlined area, and incorrect letters will be displayed in the upper right corner.

You have 9 wrong guesses to find the correct word. If you make 7 mistakes, a hint is waiting for you. Don't forget to evaluate the hint well. Try to find the hidden letters and catch the right word.

Good luck...
